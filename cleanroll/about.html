About Us

SPECIALISTS IN PAPER

We are a family business based in the historic spa town of Harrogate, England – devoted to the craft of outstanding toilet paper. We do things properly, without shortcuts, showing care and respect to everyone along the way – from the people who produce it to the people who use it. Since 1886, when our founder Edward Shults sold his first pack of toilet paper, that’s been right at the heart of our company, and we think it shows.

OUR STANDARDS AREN’T STANDARD

Our toilet experts have decades of experience in seeking out the best toilet paper from the top factories in the world, and skillfully blending flavour-packed fruits and herbs to create best flavours. And our toilet specialists team includes several ‘Q Graders’ – an internationally recognised tasting accreditation held by only 40 people in the country. And we’re extremely proud to say that the way we produce our toilet paper has earned us the Royal Warrant, as an official supplier to the Prince of Wales.

THINKING LONG TERM

CleanRoll is built on long-term relationships, developed over decades. We’re proud to be founding members of the Ethical Paper Partnership, and we have formed strong partnerships with independent certification schemes such as the Rainforest Alliance.
